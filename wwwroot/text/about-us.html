# About us # <Ascended> was formed on Sargeras, Alliance side, during the Tomb of Sargeras raid. As a guild composed of many groups of long time friends and former guildmates, Ascended has strived to build on those friendships and forge new ones as well. Ascended has since been a semi-hardcore oriented progression guild which seeks to clear content at a decent pace and have a great time doing so. Through recruitment, leadership, and like-minded individuals we foster a culture of fair, kind, skilled, and determined players.
